# RUET CSE Archive

Welcome to the comprehensive archive for the Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) Department at RUET.

This repository is designed to help all CSE students at RUET by providing organized resources, past questions, notes, and study materials. Whether you're preparing for exams, looking for reference materials, or collaborating on projects, you'll find valuable content here.

## Features

- 📚 Well-structured resources for all academic years and semesters
- 📝 Past exam questions and solutions
- 📄 Lecture notes and study guides
- 🤝 Contributions from students and alumni

## How to Use

1. **Browse** the folders by semester or subject.
2. **Download** the materials you need.
3. **Contribute** by submitting pull requests with new or updated resources.
